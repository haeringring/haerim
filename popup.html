About This Page

The page is a special place to organize and introduce various pop-up spaces,
which are often held in the vibrant Seongsu area of Seoul, by district.
Seongsu is known for its unique culture and creative atmosphere,
and new pop-up events and stores are being held to provide a fresh experience to visitors.
The page divides Seongsu into three main sections and guides the characteristics and attractions of each section in detail.

The first zone is the Seongsu main area,
which is the center of large pop-ups that exude a relaxed atmosphere.
The second zone, Seongsu-Ttukseom, is a trendy space that is rising hot,
with a variety of pop-ups frequently taking place.
Lastly, the Seoul Forest area is a healing space in harmony with nature,
where pop-ups and cultural events that can be enjoyed with trails in the woods are often held.

Use this page to easily find colorful pop-up spaces in the Seongsu area and explore the unique charms of each area
